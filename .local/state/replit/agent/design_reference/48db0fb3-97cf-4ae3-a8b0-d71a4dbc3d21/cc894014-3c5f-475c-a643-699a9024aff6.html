About KBE

Empowering young minds to become the next generation of scientists and researchers through innovative competitions and learning experiences.

Vision

To ignite curiosity and inspire a global generation of young minds to explore science, innovate boldly, and shape the future of research.

Mission

To create an engaging, world-class platform for students through quizzes, expert sessions, and real-world experiences in science and research.

Goals

To discover young talent and provide life-changing opportunities including ISRO visits, scholarships, and scientific equipment.
